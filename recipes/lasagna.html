Vegetarian Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Plant-based mince
  • Two onions
  • Celery
  • Two garlic cloves
  • A cup of red wine
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • As many vegetables you like (spinach, mushrooms, aubergine, among others are good options)

Step-by-Step

  1. Have all the veggies chopped in mirepoix.
  2. Heat a pan, then add oil and the plant-mince until golden brown.
  3. Add and sauté veggies until golden and soft, then add a splash of red wine to deglaze the bottom.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes, when boiling add the rest of red wine and let simmer for around 20 minutes in lower heat.
  5. While sauce is cooked, put the lasagna sheets in boiling salted water for around 5 minutes until soft.
  6. On a baking dish, add a dash of olive oil and then layers of sauce, cheese and lasagna sheets, in that specific order.
  7. Cover the baking dish with foil wrap, and put in a 180C oven for around 15 minutes. Once finished, uncover and let the cheese melt and burnt a bit for other 10 minutes.
  8. Once you feel the lasagna sheets are soft, put aside to rest for 20 minutes to settle.
  9. Now, you're ready to taste your beautiful vegetarian lasagna
